The whipping boy is the story of a boy who has to be whipped harshly when ever the prince does some thing wrong.The Boys name is Jermmy his father sells rats. Prince brat who lived up to his name. He got the whipping boy whipped a great deal and was far more intelligent then the prince. One day the prince decided to run away from the castle so he brought Jermmy with him he was going to let him go free but the prince knowing that he would be cought and killed but before he decided what to do him and the prince were captured by hold your nose Billy and Cutwater. Jermmy pretended to be the prince and wrote a letter to the king and would send his "whipping boy" the prince to the castle. They ended up running as fast as they could from the kidnapers and were saved when a bear crossed the kinapers path and not thier's. They met the owner of the bear who was looking for her. The two boys walked gathering sticks to sell for there future meal. They ran into a hot potato man who would have lent them a ride when the kidnapers found them again. The prince was whipped thinking that they were whipping the whipping boy. To their supprise the owner of the danceing bear they met before was let lose on the kidnapers. The hot potato man, the bear it's owner, the prince and the whipping boy all went to the city to gain money for their meals. They all earned money, ate and met old friends well at leased Jermmy did. Jermmy cought many rats but at the end of the day he still had most of them. Prince brat found out that most of his subjects hated him and called him Prince brat. The boys found out that they had a price on their head for who returned them to the casle so they tried to sneak into the gutter. Billy and Cutwater looked for them and almost captured them when they were attacked by a pack of rats from Jermmy's cage. Prince brat had an idea he was going to let the owner of the bear and the potato man have the money for captureing them. He also personally tried his best to be nice to Jermmy and not have him whipped.
